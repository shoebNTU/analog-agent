Runnable but idealized RFPA topology seed.

This template maps the RFIC textbook's differential cascode/neutralized PA
archetype into the existing single-ended RFPA testbench by using an idealized
passive coupled-inductor transformer seed. It is useful for topology exploration
and parser testing, but a real transformer/balun model, common-mode checks, and
extracted passives are required before treating results as design evidence.
